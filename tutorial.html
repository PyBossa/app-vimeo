Vimeo Search tutorial

Hi! This is a demo application that shows how you can do pattern recognition on video using the PyBossa framework in Crowdcrafting.org.

The application is really simple. It loads a video from Vimeo and asks you this question: Is this video showing a scientific experiment?

The first lane of Nerdy Derby from Tak Cheung on Vimeo.

You will have 3 possible answers:

  • Yes,
  • No, and
  • I don't know

All you have to do is to click in one of the three possible answers and you will be done. This demo application could be adapted for more complex pattern recognition problems.
